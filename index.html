Welcome to Wall Dash

A fast-paced, neon-infused arcade dodging game designed to test your reflexes and timing.

About Wall Dash

Wall Dash is a fast-paced neon arcade game. Survive by dodging crystal spikes as the speed continuously increases.

How to Play

Tap the screen, Click your mouse, or press Spacebar to switch walls and dodge the incoming obstacles. Timing a dodge at the very last second before impact grants a "PERFECT!" rating, dramatically increasing your score multiplier.

Features

  • Responsive Controls: Instant lane switching with zero delay, optimized for both desktop and mobile.
  • Premium Visuals: Experience glowing neon graphics, particle bursts, and a multi-layered parallax background.
  • Dynamic Difficulty: The speed and intensity of the game automatically scale up the longer you survive.
