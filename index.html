About Wall Dash

Wall Dash is a fast-paced neon arcade game. Survive by dodging crystal spikes as the speed increases.

How to Play

Tap, Click, or press Spacebar to switch walls and dodge. Timing a dodge at the last second grants a "PERFECT!" score multiplier.

Features

  • Responsive Controls: Instant lane switching with zero delay.
  • Dynamic Scaling: Game speed increases the further you go.
